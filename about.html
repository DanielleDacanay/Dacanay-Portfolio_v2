Business Analyst

As a full-time Business Analyst on an Agile government project, I regularly engage with our clients to determine underlying business problems, gather requirements and propose innovative solutions that meet their expectations. In additon, I serve as a Scrum Master, Product Owner and Tester as needed. As part of regular release activities, I maintain documentation such as requirement specifications, conduct Sprint demos for the client and facilitate user acceptance testing.


UX Researcher & UI Designer

With each project release, I continuously learn how client interacts with the system and determine ways to improve the system to fit their needs while enhancing the user experience. Prior to development, I create visual workflows and mockups to help my Scrum team and the client converge onto one vision. To broaden and strengthen my understanding of the user experience, I am currently part of a FedTech case study under the ACT-IAC Customer Experience (CX) Community of Interest.
